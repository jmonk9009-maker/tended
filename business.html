How it works

Three steps to verified maintenance

From upload to insight in under a minute.

Features

Built for portfolios, not single assets

Everything you need to manage maintenance at scale.

Portfolio Dashboard
Real-time health scores across all assets in one table view. Sort by score, filter by overdue, drill into any property.
AI Receipt Verification
Upload a receipt, AI reads it, verifies it, categorizes it instantly. Dual-layer OCR plus vision analysis for maximum accuracy.
Multi-Unit Properties
Track trailer parks, duplexes, apartments -- each unit independently. One portfolio, every unit accounted for.
Spending Analytics
Charts showing monthly spend, cost per property, spending trends. Know where your money goes before tax season.
Verified Tended Reports
Shareable proof of maintenance for sales, insurance, or disputes. Every entry backed by documentation, not just a log.
Cash and Manual Entries
Not every job has a receipt. Log cash payments with photos of work done. Honest records build credible reports.
